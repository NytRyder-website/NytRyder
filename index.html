Nyt Ryder Policy

1. Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and protect your personal information. Your data is stored securely and never shared with third parties without your consent.

2. Ride Safety

All drivers are vetted and background checked. Your safety is our priority. In case of emergency, please contact local authorities.

3. Cancellation Policy

You may cancel within the first minute of booking without penalty. After that, cancellation fees may apply based on driver response time.

4. Payment Terms

Payments are processed securely. Cash payments are accepted with change calculation. Card payments are processed via Speedpoint.
